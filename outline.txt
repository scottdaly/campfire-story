THE LONG PORTAGE — outline

Three friends and a dog take a three-day canoe trip to find the
campsite from a photograph none of them remembers taking.

Ch 1 — Put-in and first camp. A storm arrives early.
Ch 2 — The morning after. The photograph is missing.
Ch 3 — The long portage. The map lies. The photograph turns up
       where nobody looks.
Ch 4 — TBD: do they ever find the campsite, and does it matter?
